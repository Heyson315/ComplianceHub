## 📋 **How to Get QBOA (Free)**

### **Step-by-Step:**

```yaml
Step 1: Visit QuickBooks Accountants Portal
  URL: https://quickbooks.intuit.com/accountants/
  
Step 2: Click "Sign Up"
  Select: "QuickBooks Online Accountant"
  
Step 3: Verify Your Accountant Status
  Options:
    ✅ CPA license number
    ✅ AICPA membership  
    ✅ Accounting firm email (@cpa-firm.com)
    ✅ ProAdvisor certification (can get free during signup)
  
Step 4: Instant Access
  Result: FREE account activated immediately
  Features: Full API, 30 client slots, unlimited sandbox
```

### **Verification Methods:**

**Option A: CPA License (Easiest)**
- Enter your state CPA license number
- Instant verification (if in Intuit database)

**Option B: AICPA Membership**
- Enter AICPA member ID
- Verifies through AICPA database

**Option C: Accounting Firm Email**
- Use email from recognized CPA firm domain
- Example: hassan@hhr-cpa.com ✅

**Option D: ProAdvisor Certification**
- Get FREE during QBOA signup
- Takes ~2 hours (online training + test)
- Adds credibility + unlocks features
